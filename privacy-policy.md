# Eazybizflow Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 3, 2026

Thank you for choosing Eazybizflow. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use our services to manage campaigns, automate social posts, capture leads, and track sales.

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Account information:** name, email, company name, billing details.
- **Content and campaign data:** posts, campaign settings, lead lists, sales records, analytics, and other content you upload or generate using our services.
- **Usage data:** logs, feature usage, IP address, device and browser information, timestamps.
- **Cookies and similar technologies** to enable features and improve your experience.

## 2. How We Use Information
- Provide and maintain services (campaign management, automation, lead capture, sales tracking).
- Process payments and manage your account.
- Improve, analyze, and personalize the product and features.
- Communicate with you about updates, billing, and support.
- Detect, prevent, and respond to fraud, abuse, or security incidents.

## 3. Sharing and Disclosure
- **Service providers:** we may share data with trusted vendors who perform services on our behalf (hosting, analytics, payment processors).
- **Integrations and third-party platforms:** when you connect third-party services (social networks, CRMs), we may share data necessary for those integrations.
- **Legal and safety:** we may disclose information to comply with legal obligations or to protect rights, property, or safety.
- **Business transfers:** if Eazybizflow is merged, acquired, or otherwise reorganized, data may be transferred as part of that transaction.

## 4. Your Choices and Controls
- **Account settings:** update or remove account information from your account dashboard.
- **Integrations:** you control which third-party integrations are connected and can revoke access at any time.
- **Cookies:** you can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

## 5. Data Security
We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect data. However, no system is completely secure; avoid sharing highly sensitive personal data unless necessary.

## 6. Data Retention
We retain personal data as long as it is necessary to provide services and as required by law. You may request deletion of your account and related data; some backups or logs may persist for a limited time.

## 7. International Transfers
Eazybizflow may transfer and store information on servers located in different countries. By using our services you agree to the transfer and processing of your information in those locations.

## 8. Children
Our services are not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## 9. Changes to this Policy
We may update this policy; we’ll post changes and update the Effective Date. Material changes will be communicated via email or in-product notice.

## 10. Contact
For questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, contact: privacy@eazybizflow.com
